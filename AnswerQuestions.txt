Activity 2

1. How can you make pink?
  Have every slider at max except the Green one(which should be lower). For example, Red: 255, Green: 181, Blue: 255.
2. How can you make yellow?
   Have every slider at max except blue. For example, Red: 255, Green: 255, Blue: 159.
3. How can you make purple?
  Have green the lowest value of the sliders. Red should be set at higher than green but lower or equal to the value of the blue slider. The blue slider must be greater than or equal to the green slider. For example, Red: 100, Green: 36, Blue: 159.
4. How can you make white?
  Set every slider to 255.
